About

I’m a PhD student in the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, a Hong Kong PhD Fellowship Scheme awardee. My major is Mechanical (officially) Aerospace Engineering with a concentration in scientific computation. This blog is to share some knowledge about my discipline and hopefully you may find it helpful for your research as well. This blog tries to not repeat those information available everywhere else on the website, but to provide some comments based on my research experience. I also try my best to make it easy to read, even for those not from science or engineering background.
